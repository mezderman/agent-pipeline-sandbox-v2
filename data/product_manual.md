# Smartwatch X200 Troubleshooting Guide

## Unresponsive Screen
- Press and hold the power button for 10 seconds to force restart.
- Ensure the screen is clean and free from moisture.
- Check for any pending software updates in the companion app.

## Charging Issues
- Use the original charger and try a different power outlet.
- Clean the charging ports with a dry cloth to remove dust or debris.
- Leave the watch on the charger for at least 30 minutes before powering on.
